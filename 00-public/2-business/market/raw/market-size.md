## Executive Summary

The China corporate training market is valued at approximately USD 58.3B (2025), with the AI-specific segment still nascent at ~USD 1.5-2B globally but growing at 25%+ CAGR. For 绚星 (Radnova/YXT) with ~1,700 accessible enterprise clients and an AI-native training product, the SOM is estimated at RMB 170-510M (USD 23-70M) over a 3-year ramp. The market opportunity is real but faces headwinds from enterprise training budget tightening and the challenge of proving ROI — which, paradoxically, is exactly the problem AI Teacher aims to solve.

## 9. Key Takeaways for AI Teacher Business Planning

1. **The market is real and large.** China's corporate training market at ~RMB 400B provides massive TAM. Even the narrow AI-powered training delivery SAM of ~RMB 2-3B is large enough to build a significant business. [Estimate]

2. **Timing is right.** <5% AI-native adoption globally + Josh Bersin calling this a category disruption + 25% CAGR in AI corporate training = classic early-market opportunity. [Data]

3. **The embedded distribution advantage is decisive.** With 1,700 accessible enterprise clients and a shared sales force, AI Teacher's CAC should be dramatically lower than a standalone startup. This is the single strongest competitive moat. [Opinion]

4. **Year 1 revenue target of RMB 13-16M is achievable but ambitious.** It implies ~100+ enterprise clients adopting a brand-new product category within months of launch. The 60 angel clients provide a reasonable pipeline. [Estimate]

5. **The biggest risk is not competition but market contraction.** Enterprise training budgets are under pressure. AI Teacher must prove ROI fast — which fortunately is its core value proposition. [Opinion]

6. **Unit economics look favorable on paper** (LTV:CAC potentially 6-15x due to embedded sales), but the project-based pricing model creates revenue volatility compared to subscription SaaS. Consider hybrid pricing (base subscription + project fees). [Opinion]

7. **YXT's AI MRR (RMB 500K/month, growing 150% YoY) is still tiny** relative to total revenue (RMB 331M/year). AI Teacher needs to grow 10-20x within 2 years to become material to the parent company's financials. [Data + Estimate]
